# GigShield Participant Intro Video Script

**Target length**: 30-60 seconds
**Format**: Webcam recording (face to camera)
**Tone**: Confident, concise, enthusiastic but not hype-driven

---

## Script Template

> "Hi, I'm **[Your Name]**, and I'm building GigShield.
>
> Freelancers lose billions every year to late payments, ghosting clients, and platforms that take 20 percent just to hold the money in the middle. The tools that exist today are either too expensive, too slow, or require you to trust a company that doesn't have your interests at heart.
>
> GigShield is a trustless escrow platform built on Conflux eSpace. Clients lock USDT0 into milestone-based escrows. Freelancers submit work, clients approve, and funds release instantly — all on-chain, all verifiable.
>
> If a client goes silent, a 7-day auto-release timer ensures the freelancer gets paid. If there's a real disagreement, a 3-arbitrator panel votes on-chain to resolve it.
>
> And here's what makes it work for small contracts: Conflux's gas sponsorship means every transaction is free for users. A $100 freelance gig gets the same protection as a $10,000 one.
>
> GigShield. Trustless escrow for the independent workforce.
>
> Thank you."

---

## Delivery Notes

- **Pace**: Aim for a natural conversational speed. Do not rush. A 45-second delivery at a comfortable pace is better than 60 seconds of fast talking.
- **Eye contact**: Look directly into the camera, not at a script on screen. Practice the key points enough to speak from memory.
- **Key beats to hit**:
  1. The problem (freelancer payment risk) — 1-2 sentences
  2. The solution (milestone escrow + auto-release + arbitration) — 2-3 sentences
  3. The Conflux advantage (gas sponsorship for small contracts) — 1 sentence
  4. The tagline — 1 sentence
- **What to skip**: Do not get into technical details (Solidity version, specific libraries, contract architecture). Save that for the demo video.
- **Background**: A clean, simple background. Avoid clutter or distracting elements.
